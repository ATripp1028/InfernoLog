# InfernoLog — Community Policy

Welcome to InfernoLog. This policy applies to all user-generated content on the platform, including level notes, profile bios, list names, and any other text you submit.

---

## What InfernoLog Is

InfernoLog is a personal logging tool for Geometry Dash demon completions. It is built for the GD community, by someone in the GD community. The goal is to give players a better home for their records than a spreadsheet.

---

## Content Standards

### What Is Allowed

InfernoLog is a space for honest personal records and opinions. This includes:

- Venting about levels you found frustrating, unfair, or unenjoyable
- Critical opinions about level design, decoration, or song choice
- Foul language used to express genuine feelings about a level
- Negative commentary directed at a level (not a person)

We understand that beating a brutal demon can inspire strong feelings. Level notes are your personal log, and we are not here to sanitize your experience.

### What Is Not Allowed

The following content will result in moderation action:

- **Threats of violence** against any person or group
- **Slurs** of any kind — racial, ethnic, religious, sexual, gender-based, or targeting any other protected characteristic
- **Targeted harassment** of specific individuals — creators, other players, or anyone else
- **Content that attacks a person based on** race, ethnicity, religion, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected status
- **Spam or abuse of platform features**, including submitting reports with no legitimate basis

The distinction is between expressing how a **level** made you feel versus attacking a **person**. The former is human and expected. The latter is not welcome here.

---

## Reporting

If you see content that violates this policy, you can report it using the in-app report button on any profile.

Reports are reviewed by the moderation team. Submitting a report in bad faith — to harass another user or waste moderators' time — is itself a violation of this policy and will result in account action against the reporter.

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- A formal warning
- A temporary suspension
- A permanent ban

Severity and history are taken into account. The moderation team aims to be consistent and fair.

---

## Appeals

If your account is banned and you believe it was in error, you may submit one appeal through the appeals portal. Appeals are reviewed by a moderator. The decision on an appeal is final.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the platform grows. Significant changes will be communicated to users.
